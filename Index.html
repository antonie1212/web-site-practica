Our Best Rooms

Room Nr.100

Love Room

This romantic suite is designed for newlyweds or couples celebrating special occasions. It usually features luxurious amenities such as a king-sized bed, a private balcony, a Jacuzzi or a hot tub, and intimate decor.

Room 202

Family Room

The family suite is designed to accommodate families or groups. It typically features multiple bedrooms, a shared living area, and amenities suitable for children, such as a game console, bunk beds, or a play area.

Room 030

Relax Room

The spa suite offers a tranquil oasis for guests looking to relax and rejuvenate. It often includes a private spa or a dedicated area for spa treatments, such as a massage table or a steam room, along with plush bedding and serene decor.

Room 444

Presidential Room

The presidential suite is the epitome of luxury and grandeur. It boasts expansive living spaces, multiple bedrooms, a private dining area, and panoramic views. It often comes with personalized services, such as a private butler or a dedicated chauffeur.

Room 005

Luxury Villa

The luxury villa is a standalone accommodation option, separate from the main hotel building. It offers utmost privacy and exclusivity, featuring a private pool, a garden, a fully-equipped kitchen, and spacious living areas. It is perfect for guests seeking an opulent retreat.

Room 660

Underwater Room

The underwater suite is a truly unique and immersive experience, allowing guests to stay in an underwater chamber with large windows offering stunning views of marine life. It provides an extraordinary opportunity to observe the underwater world while enjoying luxurious amenities.

Room 700

Art Room

The art suite is a haven for art enthusiasts, with every aspect of the room designed to showcase artistic elements. It may include hand-painted murals, sculptures, unique furniture, and a gallery-like ambiance that immerses guests in a creative environment.

Room 888

Gym Room

The gym room is a private space within the hotel designed to provide guests with a convenient and well-equipped fitness facility. It typically features a range of exercise equipment such as treadmills, stationary bikes, weightlifting machines, and free weights.
